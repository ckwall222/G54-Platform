⏱ Event-based cadences. Define what happens when a lead reaches a state — e.g. contact info captured, then several days pass with no logged activity. Each step waits for a time or event, checks a condition, then fires an action: an automated email, or a task/notification for the rep to make a call. This is what makes sure no lead goes cold.

Day 0
on trigger
When: lead captured / contact info provided

Day 3
if no contact
If: no call, email, or meeting logged in 3 days
Call Notify assigned rep to phone the lead
Day 5
if still cold
If: still no logged activity
Email Auto follow-up email + Task flag to sales manager
Day 10
final
If: no response
Task Mark lead dormant · schedule quarterly re-touch
Add Step
days after previous step
